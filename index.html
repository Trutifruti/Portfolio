Projects

Industrial Project, Creation of a mouvment based HM interface

During the first semester of 2021, our school tasked Eddy Abou Mansour, Antoine Montagne and myself to desing a movment controlled graphic interface that would present every internships and exchanges that the students of ISMIN had done.

For this project, we develloped a website (the graphic interface), used Nuitracks gesture recognition technology combined with a unity project and an IntelRealsens D435 camera to detect specifique gestures and procure a tochless interface to the user.

The graphic interface takes the form of an earth globe that we can spin-around, zoom-in and out of and select pins on (pins wich represent the students internships and exchanges).
